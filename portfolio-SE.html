Food Planner

Georgia Tech, US; Intro to Health Informatics ; Fall 2019

A tool to help guide people in the direction of meal planning based on current conditions. Patients conditions are automatically pulled from Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources(FHIR) and available ingredients are entered manually. Then this app provides quick, intermediate, or other meal ideas based on these factors. Recipe results can link users to full recipes as well as display some basic nutritional information.
